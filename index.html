What Pea detects

The hidden ingredients most people never notice.

Sodium Benzoate (E211)
Preservative that may form benzene when combined with vitamin C. Linked to hyperactivity.
Aspartame (E951)
Artificial sweetener classified as possibly carcinogenic by the IARC in 2023.
BHA (E320)
Synthetic antioxidant classified as a possible human carcinogen by the IARC.
Sodium Nitrite (E250)
Found in processed meats. Linked to increased risk of colorectal cancer.
Carrageenan (E407)
Thickener derived from seaweed. May cause inflammation and digestive issues.
Mono & Diglycerides (E471)
Emulsifier that may contain undeclared trans fats not shown on the label.
Canola / Rapeseed Oil
A seed oil associated with inflammation. Flagged by Pea on every scan.
High Fructose Corn Syrup
Highly processed sweetener linked to obesity, insulin resistance and fatty liver.
